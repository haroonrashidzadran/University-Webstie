About Our University

Excellence in Education Since 1931

Kabul University is Afghanistan's oldest and most prestigious institution of higher learning. Founded in 1931, we have been at the forefront of academic excellence, producing generations of leaders, scholars, and professionals who have shaped our nation's history.

Our commitment to knowledge, research, and community service has made us the cornerstone of higher education in Afghanistan, with partnerships across the globe.
